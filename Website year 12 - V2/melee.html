Primary Secondary Melee

Stock fists

Heavies stock fists the ones you get when you first boot up the game are not very useful. Heavies melees serve more of a utility purpose and not for damage dealing. Heavy has the highest DPM(Damage Per Minute) primary weapon in the game meaning having a damage dealing melee weapon is not viable. Other melees like the Fists of Steel tend to outclass the stock fists.

The Warrior's Spirit.

Heavies worst weapon due to one massive downside. 30% damage vulnerability. This means for example if a rocket were due to do a base damage of 100. With the increased damage vulnerability that rocket would then deal a massive 130 damage. Heavy is the tank class made for soaking up damage. Having an item that makes him less tanky is a no go. Avoid this weapon.

The Fists Of Steel

Heavies most useful and versatile melee weapon. It has one outstanding stat that being the -40% damage from ranged sources while active. Meaning you take 40% less damage from everything but melee weapons while you hold the fists. Allowing you to tank massive amount of damage. For example a headshot dealing 150 damage will now only do 90 damage. With a bit of practice this becomes the go to choice for many heavy players. Highly recommended choice.

The Gloves Of Running Urgently

A very strong choice for heavy players looking to be more of a mobile and flanking playstyle. Having that 30% extra movement speed while the gloves are active allows heavy to move much quicker and get to the front lines faster. I recommend using this melee if there is no teleporter present for faster travel time to the front line.

The Holiday Punch

The Holiday Punch is quite an interesting weapon and is considered more of a “joke” weapon. Meaning it's more intended for a fun time. It's a regular choice for friendly players and on joke servers. In terms of actual use it is a straight upgrade of stock but falls behind more competitive choices like the GRU and Fists of steel. Recommend for a bit of fun.

The Killing Gloves Of Boxing.

The KGB is another weapon that is a straight upgrade from the stock fists but falls behind the other weapons like the GRU and Fists Of Steel. The KGB is a powerful choice with the 5 second crits but in actual use isn’t a very strong competitive heavy choice with only one upside.

The Eviction Notice

Another fun choice weapon to use when messing around. The Eviction Notice was once a strong choice until the stats were changed and now is a downgraded version of the GRU. I don’t recommend using this weapon as other melees do its job better and more efficiently.
